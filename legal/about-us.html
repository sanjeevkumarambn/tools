About Rojgar Sangam Tools

Rojgar Sangam Tools is the official tools platform of Rojgar Sangam — India's trusted Sarkari Job and Education Information Website. Since 2017, Rojgar Sangam has been serving students and government job seekers across India with verified recruitment notifications, exam results, admit cards and educational updates.

Rojgar Sangam Tools extends this mission by providing a free collection of online utility tools that help you process documents, resize images, convert files, calculate academic results and prepare government application forms — all without paying a single rupee and without uploading your files to any server.

Every tool runs entirely inside your browser. Your files, photos and documents stay completely private on your own device. This is our core commitment to every user who visits our platform.
