How to Calculate Exact Age Online

Select your Date of Birth from the date picker.

Step 3: Calculate

Frequently Asked Questions

How to Calculate Exact Age?+

Enter your Date of Birth and the target date, then click Calculate Age. The tool shows your exact age in years, months and days along with total days, weeks and hours lived.

Can I Calculate Age for Any Past or Future Date?+

Yes, you can set any date in the Age as on Date field to calculate your age on any past or future date.

How Many Days Have I Lived?+

After calculating your age, the result screen shows your total days, total weeks, total months and total hours lived from your date of birth.

When is My Next Birthday?+

The result screen shows exactly how many days are left until your next birthday from the target date you selected.

Is This Age Calculator Free to Use?+
