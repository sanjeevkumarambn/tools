Frequently Asked Questions

CBSE CGPA to Percentage Formula Kya Hai?+

CBSE standard formula: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5. Example: CGPA 9.0 = 85.5%.

SGPA Aur CGPA Mein Kya Fark Hai?+

SGPA ek semester ka grade point hai. CGPA sabhi semesters ka weighted average hota hai.

VTU Ka Formula Kya Hai?+

VTU Karnataka formula: Percentage = (CGPA − 0.5) × 10.

Distinction Ke Liye Kitna Percentage Chahiye?+

Generally 75% aur upar Distinction hoti hai. First Class 60%–74.9%, Second Class 50%–59.9%.

GPA 4.0 Scale Ko Percentage Mein Kaise Convert Karein?+

Formula: Percentage = (GPA / 4.0) × 100. Example: GPA 3.5 = 87.5%.
